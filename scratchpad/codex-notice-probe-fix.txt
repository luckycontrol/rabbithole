4. Blur the focused action button in-page.
5. Wait another 1600ms and assert hover alone keeps it visible.
6. Move the mouse away.
7. Wait up to 4000ms for invisibility and assert dismissal.

Assertions now distinguish focus pause, hover-only pause, and mouseleave resume, including actual visibility values.
